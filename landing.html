Encoding & Decoding

Encoding

Encoding is the process of applying a specific code,
such as letters, symbols and numbers, to data
for conversion into an equivalent cipher.

Decoding

Decoding is the process of converting code into plain text
or any format that is useful for subsequent processes.
It converts encoded data files to their original states.
